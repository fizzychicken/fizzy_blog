Why fizzychicken?

While it wasn’t the first username I ever chose, I picked fizzychicken as my online gaming name some time around 1999 after hearing it used by a friend to argue about all the food at McDonalds tasting the same. While I found the conversation funny, what was handy was that, at least at the time, the term was what later became known as a Googlewhack - a search query that returns only a single result…..though my search engine of choice back then was AltaVista. That search result was an online scoreboard of the game I had been playing as that same name that day. As someone that shares a name with arguably one of the most successful Movie directors of all time, any searching for me online is lost in a bottomless sea of information about someone else, so to find something unique really stuck with me. Eventually, as the world embraced the internet, the search results for the same term were flooded with bizarre recipes and other people using it as an online name, so I decided to claim the domain before someone else did to try and capture ownership of something that was once unique to me.
